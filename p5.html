Inorganic solution

The key of hydroponics is the nutrient solution. Inorganic solution is the application of plant nutrition; it is produced to provide the best nutrients for plants even in different situations.

  • Nitrogen-> Nitrate (NO3-), Ammonium (NH4+)
  • Phosphorus-> H2PO4-, PO43-, HPO42-
  • Potassium-> Potassium (K+)
  • Calcium-> Calcium (Ca2+)
  • Magnesium-> Magnesium (Mg2+)
  • Sulfur-> Sulfate (SO42-)
  • Iron-> Fe2+, Fe3+
  • Copper-> Copper (Cu2+)
  • Manganese-> Mn2+
  • Zinc-> Zinc (Zn2+)
  • Molybdenum-> Molybdate (MoO42-)
  • Boron-> BO32-, B4O72-
  • Chloride-> Chloride (Cl-)
  • Sodium
