Organic Solution

Organic fertilizers can be used to supplement or entirely replace the inorganic compounds used in conventional hydroponic solutions. However, organic fertilizers are highly variable in their nutritional compositions. Even similar materials can differ significantly based on their source.

Examples:

  • Fishmeal
  • Animal manure
  • Cottonseed meal
